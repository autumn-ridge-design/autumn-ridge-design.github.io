Decorative Plantings

Furnishing the Space. The process of specifying and executing decorative plantings is similar to choosing and installing furniture in your home. Pots, planters, and window boxes provide choices and flexibility. Specifically designed and constructed pergolas, trellises, benches and raised beds make your outdoor space unique, and interesting. We provide a full range of carpentry services, as well as extensive experience to source exactly the right stone, clay, lead, or wooden pots and planters for your plan.
