Hardscapes and Structures

Enhancing ‘The Cube.’ Every urban landscape begins with the ‘cube’ that represents your yard. Planning patios, walls, walkways, driveways and overall topography is just like planning a room addition. Materials, traffic patterns, framing views from windows and doors, color palettes, and seasonal uses all have to factor into the plan. The use of tile, bluestone, flagstone, gravel, clay bricks and pavers, berms or timbered levels can make your exterior space a seamless extension of your interiors. Our architecture background gives us a unique familiarity with the full range of hardscape materials and techniques.

We are comfortable with larger design challenges, as well. Fire pits, fountains, pergolas, and council circles provide focal points, functional gathering spaces, and outdoor entertainment environments to enhance and beautify ‘The Cube’ that embraces your home.
