Water Features

Micro-ecologies. Ponds and waterfalls bring additional motion and interest to your landscape. Moving water, aquatic plants, and fish can provide a calming focal point to any garden. However, they are also among the most care-intensive features you can install on your property. We specialize in creating water features that minimize the maintenance problems associated with stagnation, algae growth, and plant material accumulation in pools and ponds. Our extensive experience in this area helps our designs avoid these pitfalls and maximize your enjoyment.
